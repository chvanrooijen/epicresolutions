#0
Welcome to epicresolutions.org, where we provide a clear path to action for anyone looking to make a difference. In just four simple steps, you can create a personalised list of actionable resolutions that align with your roles and interests.
Let's start by exploring the idea, and then we'll build your list together.
#1
Begin by selecting the consumer resolutions that interest you most. These are grouped by areas such as Food, Travel, and Living. Choose the actions you want to take or continue as a consumer. This will help you find other resolutions that are relevant to your lifestyle and highlight opportunities where your choices can make a real difference.
#2
Next, identify the roles that apply to you. Only roles with relevant resolutions will be shown, so you can focus on what matters most. We recognise ten key roles, and together their initials spell "EPIC": Educator, Employee or Contractor, Employer, Entrepreneur, Parent or Caregiver, Politician, Investor, Citizen, Consumer, and Child. Each role brings its own perspective and set of responsibilities. By combining these roles, you gain a broad and practical view of how you can contribute to society.
#3
After you have selected your consumer resolutions and roles, you will see a list of actionable resolutions tailored to your choices. You can either adopt this list as it is or personalise it further by excluding specific roles and resolutions. This is your opportunity to create a plan that truly fits your life and interests, making your resolution list as unique as you are.
#4
Finally, download your personalised resolution list as a PDF. Keep it for offline reference or print it as a daily reminder to take action and make a difference.
This process only takes a few minutes and results in a tailored action plan designed just for you. Ready to get started? Let's compile your personalised list together.
