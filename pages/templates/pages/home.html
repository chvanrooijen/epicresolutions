Welcome to epicresolutions.org, where we provide a clear path to action for anyone looking to make a difference. In just four steps, you can create a list of actionable resolutions that align with your societal roles and causes you care about.
Here's how it works:
#1
The first step is to select the roles you identify with, such as being an Employee, Employer, Entrepreneur, Parent, Politician, Investor, Citizen, Consumer, or Child. Each of these roles offers a unique perspective and set of responsibilities, and when combined, they provide a comprehensive view of societal engagement. Also, the name "EPIC resolutions" derives from the first letters of these roles.
#2
The next step is to select the causes you care about. Whether it's sustainability, equality and diversity, or health and well-being, some causes might resonate with you more than others. By selecting the causes you care about, you can focus your efforts on the issues that matter most to you. As Dr. Seuss wisely said Unless someone like you cares a whole awful lot, nothing is going to get better. It's not.
#3
Based on your selected roles and causes, epicresolutions.org will filter and display resolutions tailored to your interests. Choose from this list to create a personalised list of resolutions.
#4
Finally, you'll receive a personalised list of resolutions. You can download it as a PDF for offline reference or printing.
