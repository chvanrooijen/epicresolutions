#0
Welcome to epicresolutions.org, where we provide a clear path to action for anyone looking to make a difference. In just four steps, you can create a personalised list of actionable resolutions that align with your societal roles and the causes you care about.
First, let's explore the concept, and then we can put together your list.
#1
Begin by selecting your societal role or roles. This will help you find resolutions that you are uniquely positioned to act upon. We identify nine roles, and it's the initial letters of these roles that make our resolutions EPIC: Employee, Employer, Entrepreneur, Parent or guardian, Politician, Investor, Citizen, Consumer, and Child. Each of these roles offers a unique perspective and set of responsibilities, and when combined, they provide a comprehensive view of societal engagement.
#2
The next step is to select the causes you care about. Whether it's sustainability, equality and diversity, or health and well-being, some causes might resonate with you more than others. By selecting the causes you care about, you can focus your efforts on the issues or challenges in society that you feel passionate about and wish to see addressed. As Dr. Seuss wisely said Unless someone like you cares a whole awful lot, nothing is going to get better. It's not.
#3
After you have selected your roles and causes, we will present you with a list of actionable resolutions tailored to your interests. You can either adopt this list as it is or further personalise it by excluding specific resolutions.
#4
Finally, you can download your personalised list of resolutions as a PDF for offline reference or printing. Use this list as a daily reminder to take action and make a difference in the causes you care about.
Now, let's compile your list.
