About

Welcome to epicresolutions.org, where you can create a list of actionable resolutions that align with societal roles you identify with and causes you care about. The list of resolutions is growing, providing a widening path to action for anyone looking to make a difference.

All resolutions are positively phrased and follow the same, simple structure: 'As a/an [role], I [action] to [goal].' For example, an entrepreneur might see, 'As an entrepreneur, I actively promote sustainable practices to restore nature and foster biodiversity.'

It's the first letters of the roles they are associated with what makes the resolutions EPIC: Employee, Employer, Entrepreneur, Parent or guardian, Politician, Investor, Citizen, Consumer, and Child. Each role offers a unique perspective and set of responsibilities, and when combined, they provide a comprehensive view of societal engagement.

To help you follow through on your commitments, the resolutions are associated with causes. Whether it's sustainability, equality and diversity, or health and well-being, select the causes that resonate with you to prioritize and narrow down down your list.

You have the freedom to include or exclude resolutions as you like, and once you've completed your list, you can save or print it as a daily reminder.

Interested in contributing code, content, or feedback? Feel free to reach out: collaborate@epicresolutions.org.
