Vulnerabilities

EPIC resolutions is committed to providing a secure website. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us by emailing

When reporting a security vulnerability, please include the following:

  • Describe the location and potential impact of the vulnerability
  • A detailed description of the steps required to reproduce the vulnerability
  • Your name and contact information

EPIC resolutions will review your report and respond as soon as possible. We appreciate your help in keeping our website safe.
