Vulnerabilities

EPIC resolutions is committed to providing a secure website. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us via email.

When reporting a security vulnerability, please include the following details:

  1. Description of Vulnerability:

    Clearly describe the location and potential impact of the vulnerability.

  2. Steps to Reproduce:

    Provide a detailed description of the steps required to reproduce the vulnerability.

  3. Contact Information:

    Include your name and contact information so that we can reach out if needed.

EPIC resolutions will review your report and respond as soon as possible. We appreciate your help in keeping our website safe.
