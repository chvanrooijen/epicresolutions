Build Your Personal Resolution List

Welcome! This tool will help you create a personalized list of resolutions based on your role and interests.

How it works:

  1. Select Consumer Resolutions: Choose the resolutions you want to do or keep doing as a consumer, organized by domain (e.g., Food, Travel, Living).
  2. Select Your Roles: Identify the roles that apply to you. Only roles with relevant resolutions will be available for selection.
  3. Customize Your List: Fine-tune your selection by choosing which roles and resolutions to include in your final list.
  4. Download Your List: Get your personalized resolution list as a PDF for offline reference or printing.

The process takes just a few minutes and results in a customized action plan tailored to your specific roles and interests.
